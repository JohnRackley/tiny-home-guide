‚úÖ 10 Steps to Building a Tiny Home

  1. Confirm zoning and minimum dwelling requirements.
  2. Verify septic feasibility early.
  3. Ensure legal road frontage and access.
  4. Confirm utilities and connection costs.
  5. Decide on foundation vs. movable structure.
  6. Evaluate slope, drainage, and soil conditions.
  7. Work with local professionals familiar with county rules.
  8. Secure permits before major expenditures.
  9. Schedule inspections at each stage.
